Game Rules

The Judge

  • The first person to enter the game will assume the role of the Judge.
  • The Judge's board will remain blank until the other players submit their chosen cards.
  • Once the other players have submitted their choices, the Judge will choose the funniest option by clicking on it.

Players

  • Each player receives seven white cards. The goal is to pick the funniest option when combined with the black card.
  • When you choose your card for the round, click on it. It will turn blank and remain so until the Judge makes their choice.
